# Batch Sync

Batch Sync reconciles the catalog against the tenant's search surface
and any configured outbound channels (marketplaces, storefronts, ERPs).
Use it after schema changes, search reconfigurations, or to recover
from drift.

## Endpoints

<Endpoint method="POST" path="/api/b2b/pim/batch-sync/search" />
<Endpoint method="POST" path="/api/b2b/pim/batch-sync/marketplace" />
<Endpoint method="GET" path="/api/b2b/pim/batch-sync/status" />

## Body

<ParamTable
  rows={[

<Callout kind="warning" title="Impact">
  A full resync for a large tenant can take tens of minutes and produces
  significant write traffic on the search surface. Prefer a narrow{" "}
  <code>filter</code> when you know which products drifted.
</Callout>
