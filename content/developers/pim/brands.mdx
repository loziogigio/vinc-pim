# Brands

Brands are first-class entities with their own pages, logos, and SEO
metadata. Every product references a brand by its `code`.

## Endpoints

<Endpoint method="GET" path="/api/b2b/pim/brands" />
<Endpoint method="GET" path="/api/b2b/pim/brands/:code" />
<Endpoint method="POST" path="/api/b2b/pim/brands" />
<Endpoint method="PATCH" path="/api/b2b/pim/brands/:code" />
<Endpoint method="DELETE" path="/api/b2b/pim/brands/:code" />

## Data shape

- `code` — stable, URL-safe tenant-unique identifier.
- `name` — `MultiLangString`.
- `description` — `MultiLangString`, HTML-safe.
- `logo` — CDN asset reference.
- `website` — external URL displayed on the brand page.
- `seo` — `{ title, description, og_image }` per locale.
