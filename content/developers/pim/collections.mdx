# Collections

Collections are flat, manually curated product sets — typically used for
campaigns (“Spring Sale”), editorial rails (“Staff picks”), or cross-sells.
Unlike categories, collections are not hierarchical and do not drive URL
structure.

## Endpoints

<Endpoint method="GET" path="/api/b2b/pim/collections" />
<Endpoint method="GET" path="/api/b2b/pim/collections/:slug" />
<Endpoint method="POST" path="/api/b2b/pim/collections" />
<Endpoint method="PATCH" path="/api/b2b/pim/collections/:slug" />
<Endpoint method="DELETE" path="/api/b2b/pim/collections/:slug" />
<Endpoint method="POST" path="/api/b2b/pim/collections/:slug/products" />
<Endpoint method="DELETE" path="/api/b2b/pim/collections/:slug/products/:entity_code" />

## Notes

- Products are referenced by `entity_code`. A product can belong to any
  number of collections simultaneously.
- Ordering within a collection is stored as an ordered array of
  `entity_code`s; PATCH with a new order to reorder.
